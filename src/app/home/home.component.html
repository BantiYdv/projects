Meet Vinay Jaiswal, Our Founder, the heart and mind behind AVX Mediaworkz
Starting out as client servicing and then creative director in the bustling world of corporate event management, he spent a decade honing his skills before heeding the call of his first love: filmmaking.
- Vinay Jaiswal, Founder AVX Mediaworkz
Since 2008, Vinay has been the creative force behind an impressive portfolio of over 1200+ audiovisuals, lending his vision to more than 100 brands and 50+ event companies.
In 2015, Vinay ventured into the realm of advertising films. Over the past 8 years, he's directed over 135 TVCs and DVCs, for top Indian and Global brands. Some of which you might've seen making rounds on the internet and TV (Check them here: www.youtube.com/@directorvinayjaiswal). 
His work has brought him in collaboration with more than 35 celebs in Bollywood, sports, and music, such as Virat Kohli, Manoj Bajpayee, Tapsee Pannu , Sonu Nigam and many top names to reckon with in the world.
Read More
Vinay is a creative-preneur, not only the business owner of the company Kreative Fire Media but is also an accomplished Director, Producer, Song Writer, Music composer, screenwriter and creative consultant. A rare one of its kind in the country. Harnessing 25 years of industry experience, Vinay conceived AVX MediaWorkz, a solution-centric haven for event companies navigating the multifaceted world of AV content.
Today, Vinay is not only scaling AVX but also nurturing a tech startup aimed at revolutionizing the Media & Entertainment industry, foraying into the music business along with setting up mainstream feature films. When he's not weaving stories or sketching out the next big idea, Vinay is a food enthusiast, a bookworm, and the kind of person you'd want to meet for an inspiring conversation. Vinay's believes that his life is a service to others, his story isn't just about his achievements; it's about a relentless pursuit of pure intent, creativity and a drive to make a difference, one frame at a time.
